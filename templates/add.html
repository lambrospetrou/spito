notes

  • All lp.gs URLs are public and can be accessed by anyone who has the link.
  • Spitty does not look into the content of your links or text spits and only take some statistics on the spit link itself (ie. number of clicks-visits)

usage tips (click to toggle)

  • Spitty can be used like every other URL link shortener (ie. is.gd or goo.gl) in order to shrink down long URLs into something less than 20 characters that is easy to share and tell everybody.
  • Additionally, Spitty is a plain text sharing service where you can just throw any text inside and generate a super-small link in order to send it over to your friends or colleagues.
  • Specify an expiration time for your spit in order to destroy it when the given seconds have passed.
  • Spit links are immutable, thus cannot be edited after being created and when the expiration time has passed they are deleted from our database so no retrieval is possible.
